MRD platform

HeMaRD

HeMaRD is a hematologic minimal residual disease tracking platform for acute leukemias and hematologic malignancies. It combines ultra-low VAF SNV/indel detection, SNP/CNV/SV evidence, longitudinal clonal evolution, and rule-based clinical risk classification in one Dash interface.

Ultra-low VAF CNV / SV Clonal evolution Risk engine
01

MRD detection engine

Uses Mutect2, HaplotypeCaller, and VarDict with force-calling of diagnostic index variants across follow-up timepoints.

02

Clonal evolution

Tracks VAF trajectories with PyClone-VI-style clone clustering, clonal sweeps, relapse expansion, and subclonal MRD patterns.

03

CNV and structural context

Integrates copy-number and structural variant calls with SNV/indel evidence across diagnosis, response, and relapse samples.

04

Risk and longitudinal dashboard

Applies ELN 2022 AML logic, B-ALL adverse-risk rules, IKZF1plus-style evidence, and per-timepoint MRD status reports.
