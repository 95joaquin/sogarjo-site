HemaLignC

HemaLignC is a software platform under Sogarjo Life Sciences focused on genomic and transcriptomic analysis for hematologic malignancies. It is designed to support fusion detection, lineage prediction, molecular subtype classification, variant interpretation, and structured clinical-style reporting.

Fusion Detection

Multi-caller analysis for identifying relevant gene fusions from sequencing data, with emphasis on hematologic malignancy workflows.

Lineage Prediction

Integrated classification methods for assigning disease lineage using molecular signals derived from expression and related genomic data.

Molecular Subtyping

Transcriptome-driven subtype prediction for clinically relevant leukemia groups, with expansion toward broader hematologic disease applications.

Clinical Reporting

Structured software output that converts complex molecular findings into clear, standardized, decision-ready reports.
