Founding thesis

Cancer infrastructure cannot be imported into every clinical reality.

Latin America has distinct cancer biology, distinct clinical constraints, and too little purpose-built validated oncology infrastructure. Sogarjo exists to build that infrastructure layer by layer, from the data up, so underserved regions can generate, interpret, and act on their own molecular evidence.

HeMaRD

DNA-seq MRD tracking and clonal evolution for acute leukemias.

HeMaRD combines ultra-low VAF variant detection, longitudinal MRD tracking, CNV/SV integration, clonal evolution, and rule-based risk auto-classification for acute leukemias and hematologic malignancies.

The five-layer build

Software is the first layer, not the final ambition.

Sogarjo is structured as a long-term institutional build. Each layer creates data, standards, tools, and capacity that make the next layer possible.

L1
Active

HemaLignC and HeMaRD create the clinical genomics operating layer: RNA-seq classification, DNA-seq MRD tracking, reporting, cohort organization, and reproducible analysis.
